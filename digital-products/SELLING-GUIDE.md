## ETSY - Marketplace untuk Template & Printable

### Langkah 1: Buat Akun Etsy
1. Buka [Etsy.com](https://etsy.com) → Sign Up
2. Pilih "Sell on Etsy"
3. Setup shop dengan:
   - Shop name (unik, mudah diingat)
   - Shop language (English/Indonesia)
   - Country & Currency

### Langkah 2: Setup Shop
```
Shop Preferences:
├── Shop name: [YourBrand]Printables atau [YourName]Designs
├── Product type: Digital Items
├── Production partner: No
└── Copyright: I can confirm...
```

## TIPS SUKSES

### 🎯 Thumbnail & Preview Images
1. **Gunakan mockup profesional**
   - Canva mockup templates
   - Smart Mockups (smartmockups.com)
   - Placeit (placeit.net)
   
2. **3-5 preview images per produk**
   - Image 1: Mockup penuh
   - Image 2: Detail/isometric view
   - Image 3: Flat lay
   - Image 4: Text overlay penjelasan
   - Image 5: What's included

### 📸 Cara Buat Mockup Cepat (Canva):
1. Buka Canva.com
2. Search "mockup template"
3. Pilih template yang cocok
4. Upload design Anda
5. Download

### 💡 Strategi Launch

#### Week 1: Soft Launch
- Upload 1-2 produk
- Share ke sosial media
- Collect feedback

#### Week 2-4: Growth
- Upload 1 produk per hari
- Optimize titles & descriptions
- Join Etsy teams / Gumroad communities

#### Month 2+: Scale
- Bundle related products
- Create seasonal variations
- Run occasional sales

### 📊 Target Realistis

| Platform | Month 1 | Month 3 | Month 6 |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|
| Etsy | 5-10 sales | 20-50 sales | 50-100 sales |
| Gumroad | 3-5 sales | 10-20 sales | 30-50 sales |
| Shopee | 10-20 sales | 30-50 sales | 50-100 sales |

### ⚠️ Mistakes to Avoid
1. ❌ Upload template tanpa preview yang menarik
2. ❌ Harga terlalu murah (undervalue produk)
3. ❌ Tidak riset kompetitor
4. ❌ Ignore SEO (tags, keywords)
5. ❌ Tidak respond customer dengan cepat
