# 🎯 30-DAY ACTION PLAN

## WEEK 1: UPLOAD & SOFT LAUNCH

### Day 1-2: Setup & Prepare
```
□ Buka Gumroad.com dan buat akun
□ Setup profile: foto, bio, banner
□ Compress semua file produk ke ZIP:
  - canva-social-kit.zip
  - printable-planner.zip
  - ebook-workbook.zip
  - checklist-sop.zip
  - svg-clipart.zip
□ Screenshot mockup HTML jadi PNG
□ Copy deskripsi dari PRODUCT-DESCRIPTIONS.md
```

### Day 3-4: Upload Products
```
□ Upload CANVA SOCIAL KIT ($25)
□ Upload PRINTABLE PLANNER ($20)
□ Upload MINIMALISM E-BOOK ($27)
□ Upload FREELANCER SOP ($22)
□ Upload COFFEE SVG PACK ($15)
□ Review semua product page
□ Test download link
```

### Day 5-7: Soft Launch
```
□ Share ke personal Facebook
□ Share ke WhatsApp/Line groups
□ Story di Instagram personal
□ Tweet tentang launch
□ DM 5-10 teman untuk support
```

---

## WEEK 2: SALES STRATEGY

### Day 8-10: Social Media Push
```
□ Buat Instagram profesional (jika belum ada)
□ Post produk pertama (screenshot mockup)
□ Buat 5 IG stories tentang produk
□ Post di 3 Facebook groups:
  - Digital Products Sellers
  - Passive Income Indonesia
  - Content Creator Indonesia
□ Engage dengan 10 orang di comments
```

### Day 11-14: Reddit & Outreach
```
□ Post di Reddit (r/entrepreneur, r/passive_income)
□ Cold email ke 5-10 potential customers
□ Buat Pinterest account
□ Pin 10 pins dari produk
□ Target: 1-2 sales
```

---

## WEEK 3: REVIEW COLLECTION

### Day 15-18: Follow Up
```
□ Email ke semua customer: "How's it going?"
□ Request gentle review
□ Share new content di social media
□ Buat video promo (simple, HP ok)
□ Post di TikTok (kalau ada)
```

### Day 19-21: Momentum
```
□ Run mini promo (10% off, 48 hours)
□ Share testimonials yang masuk
□ Repost customer feedback
□ Target: 3-5 total sales, 2-3 reviews
```

---

## WEEK 4: OPTIMIZE & SCALE

### Day 22-25: Analyze
```
□ Cek analytics: produk mana paling laris?
□ Cek traffic source: dari mana buyer datang?
□ Optimize produk yang kurang perform
□ Double down yang best seller
□ Celebrate! 🎉
```

### Day 26-28: Expand
```
□ Pilih platform kedua (Etsy atau Shopee)
□ Setup account baru
□ Upload 2-3 produk terbaik
□ Share ke new platform
□ Setup tracking spreadsheet
```

### Day 29-30: Review & Plan
```
□ Evaluasi 30 hari: apa yang work?
□ Buat goals bulan depan
□ Continue promotion consistency
□ Target: 5+ sales, 5+ reviews ✅
```
