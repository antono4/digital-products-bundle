## Strategi Mendapatkan Reviews

### 1. Email Follow-up Otomatis

**Setup di Gumroad:**
```
Settings → Email Settings → New Purchase Email
```

**Template Email Review Request:**
```markdown
Subject: Quick question about your recent purchase 🙏

Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you so much for purchasing [Product Name]!

I hope the [product] is helping you with [benefit].

I'd love to hear your thoughts! If you have 2 minutes, 
could you leave a quick review? It really helps other 
customers make informed decisions.

[Link to product page]

Even just a few words like "Great product!" or 
"Exactly what I needed!" would be amazing.

Thanks so much for your support!

Best,
[Your Name]

P.S. Have questions or need help? Just reply to 
this email - I'm always happy to help! 💬
```

---

### 2. Timing Perfect - When to Ask

```
⏰ BEST TIME TO ASK:

Day 1: Send thank you + download link
Day 3-5: Follow up if no confirmation
Day 7-10: Request review (after they've used it)
Day 14: Second reminder (gentle)

⚠️ Jangan terlalu agresif - respect customer's time
```

---

### 3. Make It Easy

```
✅ INCLUDE DIRECT LINK:
→ Link ke halaman review/product
→ Jangan minta mereka cari sendiri

✅ GIVE OPTIONS:
→ "Can't write a review? Just reply to this email 
   and I'll add your testimonial!"

✅ OFFER GUIDANCE:
→ "Here are some things you could mention:
   - How it helped you
   - What you liked most
   - Would you recommend to others?"
```

## Cara Get First Reviews FAST

### Strategi 1: Inner Circle
```markdown
1. Friends & Family
   → "Hey, can you buy this and leave a review? 
      I'll refund you!"
   → More acceptable than you think

2. Close Network
   → Colleagues, classmates
   → People who know you personally

3. Online Friends
   → Discord, Slack communities
   → People you've interacted with
```

### Strategi 2: Exchange Reviews
```markdown
🤝 MUTUAL SUPPORT:

1. Find other new sellers
2. Buy each other's products
3. Leave honest reviews
4. Win-win!

Groups to find:
- "Digital Products Sellers" (Facebook)
- "New Seller Support" (Facebook)
- "Seller 4 Seller" (various platforms)
```

### Strategi 3: Beta Testers
```markdown
👥 BEFORE LAUNCH:

1. Offer FREE products to 5-10 beta testers
2. Get feedback + testimonials
3. Fix issues
4. Launch with initial reviews
```

## Review Best Practices

### Do's:
```
✅ Ask at the right time (after use, not immediately)
✅ Make it easy (direct link)
✅ Be genuine, don't pressure
✅ Thank every reviewer
✅ Respond to all reviews
✅ Use reviews in marketing
```

### Don'ts:
```
❌ Don't buy fake reviews
❌ Don't pressure for 5-star only
❌ Don't spam with review requests
❌ Don't ignore negative reviews
❌ Don't incentivize reviews (against Gumroad TOS)
```

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

```
❌ MISTAKE 1: Asking before customer uses product
   → WAIT 7-10 days after purchase

❌ MISTAKE 2: Spamming review requests
   → Max 2 gentle reminders

❌ MISTAKE 3: Only asking, not thanking
   → Always acknowledge reviews

❌ MISTAKE 4: Ignoring negative feedback
   → Respond professionally, improve product

❌ MISTAKE 5: Buying fake reviews
   → This violates Gumroad terms & destroys trust
```
